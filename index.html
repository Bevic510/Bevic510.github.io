About Me

I do not know what to write about myself, so I guess I'll just give you some background information. I am currently attending The New School, I'm a junior, or senior- I transferred, I'm not even sure anymore since my credits got all wonky- and I am twenty-six years old. Way too old to be still going for my bachelors, but whatever. I was an anthropology major at St.John's and was very close to graduating but things happened and now I am here as a Liberal Arts student.

ANYWAY I am really into all sorts of animals/creatures, food and writing. That's pretty much me in a nutshell. I can speak Japanese and I play a lot of video games as well as eat an unhealthy amount of hot cheetos and drink barrels worth of coffee every day.

Writing
